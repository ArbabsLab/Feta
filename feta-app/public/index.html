About Us

We are a locally-owned Greek cafe, serving authentic mediterranean cuisine every day since 2007. Our ingredients are freshly prepared and sourced local to provide the freshest meals. The owners have been rolling filo dough and wrapping gyros for over 20 years and brought their love for cooking over to America to share the beauty of Greek culture. Stop by for a quick souvlaki bite and a sip of tsipouro.
